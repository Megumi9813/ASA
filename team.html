About Us

To learn a language is to connect with the world as well as your own human capacities for personal growth. At ASA, our goal is to facilitate these connections by drawing on a broad spectrum of resources which engage our students and expand their language, their potential, and ultimately their world.

Who We Are
Graeme Cheadle
Graeme has nearly a decade of experience teaching academic English and preparing students for standardized tests, particularly in the Cambridge CEFR system (FCE, CAE, CPE) and IELTS (Academic and General), and has also taught TOEFL, SSAT, and CELPIP. After graduating with a BA in Peace and Conflict Studies from the University of Toronto, Graeme studied in Europe, where he got his MA. He then spent a year teaching in Japan as part of the JET Programme. He has also worked for the Olympics, and is a qualified career college instructor. Graeme is passionate about history, sports, and fantasy fiction, and is actively involved in politics. He strongly believes in making connections with learners and building personalized programs of study to meet each student’s unique needs.

Brockton Kuznetsov
After completing his degree in political science and philosophy, Brock headed off to South Korea where he discovered a whole new world both culturally and professionally.
