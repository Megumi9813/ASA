Frequently Asked Questions

Additional Information

Q: How long are the classes/programs?

A: Our class lengths are entirely up to our students starting with a 1-hour minimum. Our programs can range anywhere from a 4-session review all the way to a multi-year mentorship program.

Q: How much are classes?

A: Prices start at $40 Canadian dollars for one-to-one classes and are negotiable for group classes.

Q: What do I need to have for these classes?

A: All you need for our classes are a functioning computer and/or a tablet with a keyboard, hi-speed internet and a motivation to learn.

Q: Do you offer group classes?

A: Yes, we offer group classes up to a maximum of 6 students to ensure everyone receives ample attention and feedback.

Q: What is ASA's payment, cancellation and refund policy?

A: Please refer to our Terms of Service for payment, cancellation and refund policy.

Q: Can I register for classes outside of North America?

A: Yes, of course. The advantage of a virtual school is you do not need to be in a native-English speaking country to receive top-tier English instruction.

Q: Why not go to a regular academy?

A: Language acquisition is an intimate process and requires a personalized and custom-tailored approach. Furthermore, instead of vague promises we use metrics to ensure progress.

Q: Why are some online English classes cheaper?

A: The majority of online English schools do not offer true English classes. Rather, they offer lectures with no authentic communication as class size will not allow for genuine interaction.
