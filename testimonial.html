Alex S. (Germany)
Jacob's approach to teaching and his students is as unusual as it is successful. One could say: there is method to his madness.


He is able to talk with you about philosophy, current politics and classical books like "Brave New World" or "1984"; all the time, he is keeping an eye on the parts of the English language that you need to improve most.


The lessons are lively and diverse, yet focused. One homework may be about a course book's chapter, while the next homework is about a contemporary, recently seen, movie.


I wrote book reports for "Animal Farm", "Brave New World" and "1984"; as well as, a movie review for "Once upon a Time in Hollywood". He was always able to help me understand some grammatical intricacies of the books better, as we discussed the contents of the different chapters.


Additionally, we went into the details of an article in "Lapham’s Quarterly", talked, at length, about English grammar and discussed what it means to write at a higher level.


His teaching helped me reach a very high score on the Toefl iBt (116/120from an original ~100/120pts).


Since the course and opportunity he offers is hard to come by, I can highly recommend Jacob to anyone looking to improve their already decent English skills further.
